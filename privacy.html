Auto Job Applicator Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-12-28

Summary

The Auto Job Applicator extension does not collect or transmit personal data to the publisher or third parties. Resumes and form data remain on your device.

Data Collection and Use

  • Local storage only: Resumes, application counters, and autofill preferences are stored locally in your browser.
  • No transmission: The extension does not send your data to external servers.
  • Single purpose: Data is used solely to auto-fill job application forms at your request.

Sharing and Selling

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Security

Data is stored locally. Uninstalling the extension removes stored data.
